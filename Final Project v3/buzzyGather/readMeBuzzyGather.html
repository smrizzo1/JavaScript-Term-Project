Buzzy Gather is a fun, interactive game for younger players.

How to play the Buzzy Gather game:

Help Buzzy gather the flowers without getting caught by a net or poked by a stick! Use your mouse or touchscreen to move Buzzy up, down, left (backward) or right (foward) with the buttons to avoid the obstacles and collect the flowers. Collect 20 flowers to win!



Future releases with more levels and obstacles planned - stay tuned!
