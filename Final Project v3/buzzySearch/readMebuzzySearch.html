Buzzy Gather is a fun, interactive game for younger players.

How to play the Buzzy Search game:

Help Buzzy find all of the words in the list! When you find a word in the grid, click on the letters to change their color. Check the words off on the list as you find them. Find all the words to win!



Future releases with more levels and word searches planned- stay tuned!
